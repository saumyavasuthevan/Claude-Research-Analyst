## 1. Market Overview

**TAM:** USD 125.68 billion (2025 estimate) [Mordor Intelligence, 2025, SRC:mordor_eu_ecommerce_cagr_2025]

**CAGR:** Two conflicting figures from Mordor Intelligence:
- 4.25% (2025–2030), reaching USD 154.78B by 2030 [Mordor Intelligence, 2025, SRC:mordor_eu_ecommerce_cagr_2025]
- ~5.98% (2025–2030) [Mordor Intelligence, 2025, SRC:mordor_eu_ecommerce_cagr] — earlier edition of same report; figures differ; most recent retrieval (Apr 2026) returned the 4.25% figure

**Industry outlook:** The European fashion ecommerce apparel market is growing steadily, driven by platform consolidation, AI-powered personalisation investment, and the structural rise of secondhand channels, while regulatory pressure on ultra-fast fashion players is creating compliance headwinds for non-EU-domiciled operators [Mordor Intelligence, 2025, SRC:mordor_eu_ecommerce_cagr_2025].

**M&A activity (last 24 months):**
- Zalando acquired About You (DACH fashion marketplace), July 2025 [Retail Gazette, Nov 2025, SRC:retail_gazette_about_you_zalando]

**Funding rounds in this space (last 12 months):**
