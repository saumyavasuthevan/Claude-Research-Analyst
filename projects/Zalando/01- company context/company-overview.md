## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

**ASOS:**
- Strengths: Global reach, wide assortment, strong brand recognition especially in UK; 59.4M monthly site visits vs Zalando's (Similarweb, Nov 2024)
- Weaknesses: Loss-making in recent years; less logistics infrastructure in Continental Europe
- Zalando's differentiation: Superior pan-European logistics, higher profitability, stronger B2B partner ecosystem

**About You:**
- Strengths: Fashion discovery focus, strong in DACH region, social/influencer commerce
- Weaknesses: Smaller scale, less logistics depth than Zalando
- Zalando's differentiation: Scale (10x+ revenue), broader brand catalogue, established fulfilment network

**SHEIN:**
- Strengths: Ultra-low prices, fast fashion speed, Gen Z appeal
- Weaknesses: Quality perception issues, regulatory scrutiny in EU, sustainability concerns
- Zalando's differentiation: Curated multi-brand experience, sustainability credentials, premium/luxury catalogue

**H&M (online):**
- Strengths: Owned brand vertical integration, physical + digital presence, global scale
- Weaknesses: Single-brand; less marketplace variety
- Zalando's differentiation: Multi-brand marketplace model, 4,000+ brands, B2C + B2B ecosystem

### Indirect Competitors

- **Amazon Fashion:** Competes for share of wallet but lacks fashion curation and discovery experience
- **Brand.com direct (e.g., Zara.com, Nike.com):** Consumers buying direct rather than via marketplace
- **Physical retail:** Brick-and-mortar fashion remains significant for try-before-you-buy customers

### Our Positioning

**Core positioning:** "Europe's leading online multi-brand destination for fashion and lifestyle" (Nomagic press release quoting Zalando, Oct 2025)

**Differentiators:**
1. **Pan-European logistics network:** Own fulfilment centres, last-mile delivery across 25 markets
2. **Data and AI advantage:** Personalisation engine, AI-powered discovery feed, size recommendations (Zalando corporate, 2024)
3. **Partner ecosystem:** 4,000+ brands via Partner Program; B2B revenue stream via Fulfillment by Zalando
4. **Profitability trajectory:** Adjusted EBIT +46% in 2024; strong 2025 growth; €300M buyback signals financial health

## Key Terminology

**GMV (Gross Merchandise Volume):** Total value of all goods sold on the Zalando platform, including partner sales
**Adjusted EBIT:** Earnings before interest and taxes, adjusted for exceptional items — Zalando's primary profitability metric
**Partner Program:** Zalando's B2B marketplace offering allowing brands to sell directly to Zalando customers
**Fulfillment by Zalando (FbZ):** Logistics outsourcing service where Zalando handles warehousing and delivery for partner brands
**Zalando Lounge:** Members-only flash sale platform offering discounted brand items
**Discovery Feed:** AI-powered personalised fashion content feed, analogous to a social media feed for fashion discovery

## Key Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Consumer Fashion Discovery
**User:** 28-year-old urban consumer in Germany
**Scenario:** Finding new brands and outfits without visiting multiple brand websites
**Before Zalando:** Visiting 5–10 brand websites, inconsistent sizing, no unified returns process
**After Zalando:** Single platform with 4,000+ brands, AI size recommendations, free unified returns
**Value:** Reduced shopping friction; one-stop fashion destination across Europe [ASSUMPTION — quantified ROI DATA UNAVAILABLE]

### Use Case 2: Brand Selling via Partner Program
**User:** E-commerce manager at a mid-size European fashion brand
**Scenario:** Expanding sales channels without building own logistics
**Before Zalando:** Limited to own website and physical retail; expensive to build EU-wide logistics
**After Zalando:** Access to Zalando's 50M+ customer base via Partner Program; optional Fulfillment by Zalando for logistics
**Value:** Accelerated European market reach without logistics capex [ASSUMPTION]

### Use Case 3: AI-Powered Personal Styling
**User:** Fashion-engaged consumer seeking outfit curation
**Scenario:** Finding items that match personal style across multiple occasions
**Before Zalando:** Manual browsing across categories; high return rates due to style mismatch
**After Zalando:** AI discovery feed surfaces relevant products; 13% increase in items added to bag observed
