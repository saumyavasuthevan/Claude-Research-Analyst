## Market Position

### Target Market

**B2C Primary:** European fashion and lifestyle consumers; 62 million active customers across 29 markets as of FY2025, served via a multi-app approach — Zalando flagship (brand-led), ABOUT YOU (trend-focused), and Lounge by Zalando (deal-driven) — each targeting distinct customer need-states [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]

**B2B Primary:** Fashion and lifestyle brands and retailers across Europe seeking e-commerce infrastructure; ZEOS (logistics), SCAYLE (shop and marketplace software), and Tradebyte (marketplace integration) serve over 1,200 merchants and enable approximately €11B in GMV as of FY2025 [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]

**B2B Emerging:** Global brands seeking cross-border e-commerce technology — SCAYLE announced a global partnership with Levi's® (powering levi.com across US, Canada and Europe) in March 2026, marking Zalando's first major US software win [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]

## Key Risks & Challenges

### Market Risks

- **Ultra-fast fashion competition and macroeconomic sensitivity:** Zalando competes against ultra-fast fashion platforms and is exposed to consumer spending sensitivity driven by inflation and disposable income trends; the company explicitly acknowledged a "fast-changing geopolitical and macro-economic environment" in its FY2025 guidance commentary [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]
- **ABOUT YOU integration execution risk:** FY2025 includes ABOUT YOU only from July 2025; delivery of the announced €100M synergy run-rate by 2028 (pulled forward one year) carries material execution risk at scale [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]

### Operational Risks

- **High structural returns rate:** Fashion e-commerce carries elevated return rates as a category; Zalando's AI Size & Fit tool is explicitly deployed to reduce size-related returns — indicating returns cost is a material operational burden [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]
- **Logistics complexity at scale:** Operating fulfilment and last-mile delivery across 29 European markets creates ongoing cost and coordination exposure; supply chain optimisation is an active area of AI investment [Zalando FY2025 Results Press Release, March 2026, SRC:zalando_fy25_results_press_release]

### Regulatory & Reputational Risks

- **EU greenwashing enforcement:** In February 2024, the European Commission required Zalando to overhaul sustainability claims on its websites, having qualified them as "misleading." Zalando committed to removing sustainability flags and vague environmental icons by mid-April 2024. [>2YR — Reuters, Feb 2024, SRC:zalando_eu_greenwashing_reuters]
- **Digital Services Act (DSA) litigation:** Zalando escalated its challenge against classification as a Very Large Online Platform (VLOP) under the EU Digital Services Act to the EU's highest court in November 2025, after a lower tribunal dismissed its initial challenge. Outcome pending as of research date. [Reuters, Nov 2025, SRC:zalando_dsa_reuters]
