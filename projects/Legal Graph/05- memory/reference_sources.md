**Tier 1 — Primary / Break-First Sources:**
- Law.com LegalTech News (law.com/legaltechnews) — broke Ironclad March 2026 Assistant launch same day
- LawNext / Bob Ambrogi (lawnext.com) — broke Kira/Litera Gen AI upgrade January 2026; reliable for legaltech product announcements
- Legal IT Insider (legaltechnology.com) — interviewed Dan Springer on Ironclad $200M ARR; covers UK and US legaltech
- PR Newswire (prnewswire.com) — all three competitors issue press releases here; search "[competitor] site:prnewswire.com"
- Artificial Lawyer (artificiallawyer.com) — covered LawGeex restructuring and Superlegal spin-off in depth

**Tier 2 — Secondary / Aggregation:**
- Above the Law — covers legal industry, occasionally breaks legaltech executive moves
- Legally Disrupted newsletter — broke (unverified) SirionLabs/LawGeex acquisition pullout story
- VentureBeat / TechCrunch — funding rounds and acquisitions
- Bloomberg Law (news.bloomberglaw.com) — covered Ironclad/Kira acquisitions and executive moves
- Yahoo Finance / Street Insider — republish PR Newswire; useful for verifying announcement dates

**Tier 3 — Review and Pricing Intelligence:**
- G2 (g2.com) — product reviews, competitor alternatives pages, CLM category page
- Capterra (capterra.com) — enterprise software reviews; useful for LawGeex and Kira sentiment
- InfoTech Research Group / McLean & Company (infotech.com/software-reviews) — enterprise buyer reviews for LawGeex
- Gartner Peer Insights — Ironclad CLM reviews; useful for mid-enterprise sentiment

**Funding/Business Intelligence:**
- Crunchbase — funding history and investor lists
- PitchBook — valuation context (subscription required; use for estimates)
- Tracxn — employee counts and funding summaries
- CB Insights — financial statements and competitor mapping
