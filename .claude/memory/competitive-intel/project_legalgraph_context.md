LegalGraph is an AI-powered legal graph and contract intelligence platform. The competitive intelligence program monitors three primary competitors: Ironclad, Kira Systems (by Litera), and LawGeex.

**LegalGraph's Core Differentiators (as established in research):**
- Graph-native architecture enabling cross-contract entity/clause relationship reasoning — a structural capability that workflow-layer AI (Ironclad's agent approach) cannot easily replicate
- Strong for heterogeneous contract portfolios — a documented weakness for Kira's extraction-model approach, which excels on uniform, high-volume contract types (M&A, real estate)
- Positions against Ironclad as "knowledge graph AI" vs. Ironclad's "workflow AI"

**Why:** These differentiators should anchor every opportunity analysis section. They define which competitor gaps are actionable for LegalGraph vs. merely informational.

**How to apply:** When surfacing opportunities, always connect back to one of these structural advantages. Avoid opportunities that require LegalGraph to compete on features it does not own (e.g., CLM workflow breadth, integrations depth).

**Competitor Segment Positions (as of March 2026):**
- Ironclad: Enterprise CLM, $200M ARR, AI-first (Jurist, Assistant, agent fleet). Primary threat.
- Kira (Litera): Law firm and Fortune 100 due diligence. Hybrid Gen AI/proprietary model. PE-backed via Litera.
- LawGeex: Enterprise contract playbook automation (F1000 focus). Post-2022 restructuring; capital constrained; split into enterprise LawGeex + independent SMB spin-off Superlegal.
